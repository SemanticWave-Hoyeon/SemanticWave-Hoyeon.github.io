## SemanticWave

**SemanticWave** is a tech-driven company leading the digital transformation of the maritime industry through AI and data analytics.

We deliver research and services across maritime IT — including vessel operational data, maritime safety information, and autonomous ship software — built on deep domain expertise.

---

### Services

- **Vessel Data Analysis** — Deep learning-based ship performance analysis including fuel consumption prediction and carbon emission estimation
- **Maritime Safety Information Processing** — NLP techniques for NAVTEX message error restoration and semantic classification
- **Maritime Data Standardization** — IHO S-100 based maritime data modeling and quality assessment
- **Autonomous Ship Software** — MASS software functional safety standards development (ISO/AWI 25928)
- **Maritime AI Software Development** — Computer vision, generative AI, and natural language processing model development
- **Maritime Cyber Security** — Vulnerability assessment, intrusion detection, and resilience strategies for shipboard OT/IT systems
